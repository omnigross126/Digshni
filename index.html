Our Story

First time we met

Feb 17 , 2025

Our marriage was arranged by our parents, and our first meeting was set at a relative’s house. That was the day we saw each other for the first time, bound by tradition yet stepping into an unfamiliar journey together.

Our Marriage Proposal

March 2, 2025

This was the day of our marriage proposal when he came to my house to meet my entire family. After completing a small Hindu ritual called Narad Rupaya, our relationship was officially fixed, and he finally proposed to me.
